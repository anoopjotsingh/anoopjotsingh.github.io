Earlylife-Education

Michael Jordan was Born on February 17, 1963 in Brooklyn, New York. Grew up in Willington, North Carolina with four siblings. He Went to Emsley A. Laney High School. His Father worked as General Electric Plant Supervisor and his mother worked at a bank. His father and mother taught him to him to always worked hard and stay away from the street life. Jordan failed to make basketball team when he was a sophomore. He worked hard made the basketball team the next year. His father was his first basketball trainer.

His father wanted him to be a mechanic at first but in his senior year Jordan played so good he got selected for All American team. After high school Michael went to University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. One of the main reasons why Michael went to UNC is because he wanted to play under legendry coach Dean Smith. Michael even considered Dean Smith to be his second father. Dean Smith meant so much to Michael Jordan. Michael Jordan helped UNC win the championship there.

They beat Georgetown town on a last second shot by Michael Jordan. Michael Jordan was never afraid to take a big shot or take on a new challenge. Michael played at UNC for two years and both of these years he was named college athlete of the year by Sporting News. Michael Jordan got drafted by the bulls in 1984 NBA draft and he got his bachelor's degree in geography in 1985.

Charity Work

Michael Jordan have made a lot of money thorough out his career but he also loves giving back. Michael Jordan havedonated more that Five million dollar to National Museum of African American History and culture. He also havedonated to charities like Make -A-Wish foundation and other Chicago area charities. He was also named the new "Chief Wish Ambassador" for the Make-A-Wish Foundation. Jordan will be the most prominent figure on the foundation's new Wish Ambassador Council. After 9/11 Jordan donated his salary to the victims of the attack. He also plays golf in celebrity charity tournaments that provide basketball scholarships while also donating to high schools,Habitat for Humanity, branches of the Boys and Girls Club of America, and other worthwhile projects.

In 2017 Novant Health announces a $7 million gift from Jordan to open two Novant Health Michael Jordan Family Clinics in Charlotte, North Carolina. Jordan Loves donating to anybody in need but he has special place in his heart for the people of North Carolina and Chicago. That is why Jordan opened up two medical clinics he has funded in Charlotte, North Carolina.The facility offers care to Charlotte residents, including underinsured and uninsured patients.

In 1994, Michael Jordan founded the James R. Jordan Boys and Girls Club and Family Life Center to honor the memory of his father who died in 1993. Michael Jordan provided a personal donation of $2 million to get the organization up and running after its creation. In 1996, Jordan also founded the Jordan Institute for Families at the University of North Carolina where he played college basketball. The Jordan Institute for Families develops policies that aid in bringing families closer together while engaging the entire community.

Jordan also donated millions of dollars to provide relief for the people that were affected by natural disasters. Jordan donated $1 million to Hurricane Dorian relief efforts in the Bahamas. In 2006, Jordan Brand donated $450,000 to Habitat for Humanity. The organization used the money to rebuild New Orleans after Hurricane Katrina devastated the area in 2005 and Jordan also donated Two million Dollars toward the Hurricane Florence relief effort. This is just some of the work that Jordan have done over the years. There is so much more work he has that does not get reportedbecause Jordan does not like to talk about his charity work
